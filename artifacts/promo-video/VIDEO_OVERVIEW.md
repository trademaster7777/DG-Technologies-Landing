# D2G Technology Promo Video

## Overview
30-45 second animated promotional video for D2G Technology, pitching their phone-based website building service to small business owners.

## Creative Direction

**Aesthetic:** Cinematic Tech Product  
Dark premium look with electric blue (#3B9BF6) → violet (#A855F7) gradient accents on near-black (#0A0A0F) backgrounds, matching the established brand identity from the D2G Technologies landing page.

**Typography:** Manrope (brand font) - 800 weight for headlines, 600 for body

**Motion Language:** Smooth ease curves, crisp transitions, elegant reveals. Premium and confident pacing - never jarring, always intentional.

**Visual System:**
- Animated gradient orbs (blue/violet) that drift and pulse
- Glass card effects with subtle glows
- Staggered word/element reveals
- Cross-scene continuity via persistent background elements

## Scene Breakdown

**Total Duration:** ~28.5 seconds (perfect for social media/web)

1. **Scene 1 - Hero** (3.5s)
   - D2G logo reveal
   - Staggered headline: "Your Website is One Phone Call Away"
   - Tagline: "Professional websites for small business"
   - Animated gradient orbs background

2. **Scene 2 - Problem** (4.5s)
   - "You're great at what you do. But building a website?"
   - "There's a simpler way."
   - Accent line draw animation

3. **Scene 3 - Process** (5.5s)
   - Three-step process with sequential reveals:
     1. 30-minute strategy call
     2. We build your draft
     3. You launch
   - Phone call illustration in background
   - Animated step indicators with gradient fills

4. **Scene 4 - Benefits** (3.5s)
   - "Why choose D2G?"
   - Three benefits with checkmark reveals:
     - Done entirely over the phone
     - Launched in days, not months
     - You own it — no lock-in
   - Tech connection imagery background

5. **Scene 5 - Pricing** (4.5s)
   - "Simple, flat-fee pricing"
   - Two pricing cards:
     - The Launchpad: $999 (single-page)
     - The Presence: $1,499 (3-page) - featured with "MOST POPULAR" badge
   - Website mockup in background

6. **Scene 6 - CTA** (4.5s)
   - "Book Your Free Strategy Call"
   - "30 minutes. No commitment. Real results."
   - Visual CTA button: "Get Started Today"
   - "Book at D2GTechnology.com"
   - Celebration burst background

7. **Scene 7 - Outro** (2.5s)
   - D2G logo lockup
   - Tagline reprise
   - Clean fade to prepare for loop

## Technical Notes

- 16:9 aspect ratio, viewport-relative sizing
- All animations use `transform` and `opacity` for 60fps performance
- Noise texture overlay for depth
- AnimatePresence with `mode="popLayout"` for seamless transitions
- Loops cleanly back to Scene 1
- No interactive elements (pure video playback)

## Key Features

✓ Brand-accurate color palette and typography  
✓ Smooth cross-scene transitions with no black gaps  
✓ Staggered intra-scene choreography (not a slideshow)  
✓ Visual hierarchy guides the viewer's eye  
✓ Real pricing data (no invented testimonials/phone numbers)  
✓ Clear CTA without interactive buttons  
✓ Professional production quality matching agency standards
